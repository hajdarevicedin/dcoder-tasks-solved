There are N students in a class and teacher want to divide these students
into some groups. teacher told that groups consisting of two or less students
not allowed, so teacher want to have as many groups as possible.

Divide the students so that the number of groups consisting of three or more students
is maximized.

INPUT: 
	Single integer N

OUTPUT: 
	Maximum number of groups can be formed.

CONSTRAINTS: 
	1<=N<100000

SAMPLE INPUT: 
	6

SAMPLE OUTPUT: 
	2
